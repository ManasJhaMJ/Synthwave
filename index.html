Synthwave

All about it.

Synthwave, an homage to the '80s and '90s, echoes retro-futurism with its pulsating synths and nostalgic melodies. It resurrects an era of arcade games and cyberpunk aesthetics, immersing listeners in neon-drenched landscapes.

Inspired games like "Hotline Miami" and "Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon" capture this vibe with vibrant colors and pulsating soundtracks, while synthwave songs like Kavinsky's "Nightcall" and Perturbator's "Venger" blend '80s pop nostalgia with modern electronic beats, defining the genre's allure.
